Best Time to Visit Bali

Weather

Bali has a tropical climate with two seasons. Dry season (April-October) brings sunny weather, while rainy season (November-March) brings monsoon rains.

Best Months

April to October offers the best weather with dry conditions.

Peak Season

June to August brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

November to March offers lower prices and fewer crowds, though with more rain.
