Best Time to Visit Boracay

Weather

Boracay has a tropical climate with two seasons. Dry season (November-April) is hot and sunny, while wet season (May-October) brings rain and humidity.

Best Months

November to April offers the best weather with less rain and ideal beach conditions.

Peak Season

December to February brings peak tourist season with perfect weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

May to October offers lower prices but frequent rain and potential typhoons.
