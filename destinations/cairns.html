Best Time to Visit Cairns (Great Barrier Reef)

Weather

Cairns has a tropical climate with two main seasons. Dry season (May-October) is pleasant, and wet season (November-April) brings rain and humidity.

Best Months

May to October offers the best weather with pleasant temperatures and less rain.

Peak Season

June to August brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

November to April offers lower prices but wet weather and potential cyclones.
