Best Time to Visit Chiang Mai

Weather

Chiang Mai has three seasons. Cool season (November-February) is pleasant, hot season (March-May) is very hot, and rainy season (June-October) brings monsoon rains.

Best Months

November to February offers the best weather with cooler temperatures.

Peak Season

November to February brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

June to October offers lower prices and fewer crowds, though with more rain.

Walking is perfect for exploring the Old City. Songthaews (shared taxis) and organized tours provide transportation to other areas.
