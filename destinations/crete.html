Welcome to Crete, where ancient Minoan civilization meets stunning natural landscapes and authentic Greek island culture. From the historic Palace of Knossos to dramatic gorges and pristine beaches, from traditional villages to modern cities, this diverse island offers the perfect blend of history, nature, and Mediterranean charm.
