Best Time to Visit Exuma

Weather

Exuma enjoys tropical weather year-round with average temperatures of 82°F (28°C). The region has pleasant trade winds and crystal-clear waters.

Best Months

December to April offers the best weather with minimal rainfall, perfect water visibility, and ideal conditions for all water activities.

Peak Season

December to April brings peak tourist season with higher prices but guaranteed excellent weather and minimal hurricane risk.

Off Season

May to November offers lower prices and fewer crowds, though June to November is hurricane season with occasional storms.

  • Compass Cay - Nurse sharks and pristine beaches

  • Little Exuma - Secluded beaches and authentic Bahamian life

Getting Around Exuma

Use organized boat tours, rent a boat, or take water taxis between islands. Many tours include transportation and equipment.
