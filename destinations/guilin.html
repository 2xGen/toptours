Best Time to Visit Guilin

Weather

Guilin has four distinct seasons. Spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) are pleasant, summer (June-August) is hot and humid, and winter (December-February) is cool.

Best Months

March to May and September to November offer the best weather with pleasant temperatures.

Peak Season

April to May and September to October brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

December to February offers lower prices but cooler weather.
