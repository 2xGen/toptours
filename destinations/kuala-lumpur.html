Best Time to Visit Kuala Lumpur

Weather

Kuala Lumpur has a tropical climate with two seasons. Dry season (May-September) brings sunny weather, while rainy season (October-April) brings monsoon rains.

Best Months

May to September offers the best weather with dry conditions.

Peak Season

June to August brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

October to April offers lower prices and fewer crowds, though with more rain.
