Best Time to Visit Mallorca

Weather

Mallorca enjoys a Mediterranean climate with hot summers and mild winters. Summers are warm (75-85°F/24-29°C), winters are mild (45-60°F/7-16°C), and spring/fall offer pleasant temperatures.

Best Months

May to June and September to October offer the best weather with mild temperatures, fewer crowds, and perfect conditions for beach and outdoor activities.

Peak Season

July to August brings peak tourist season with hot weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

November to April offers lower prices and fewer crowds, though with cooler weather and some seasonal closures.
