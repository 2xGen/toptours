Best Time to Visit Manila

Weather

Manila has a tropical climate with two seasons. Dry season (November-April) is hot and sunny, while wet season (May-October) brings rain and humidity.

Best Months

November to April offers the best weather with less rain and pleasant temperatures.

Peak Season

December to February brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

May to October offers lower prices but frequent rain and high humidity.
