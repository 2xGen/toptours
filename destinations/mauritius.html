Best Time to Visit Mauritius

Weather

Mauritius has a tropical climate. Summer (November-April) is hot and humid, while winter (May-October) is warm and dry.

Best Months

May to October offers pleasant weather with less humidity and good beach conditions.

Peak Season

December to March brings peak tourist season with warm weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

April to May offers lower prices but wet weather.
