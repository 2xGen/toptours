Best Time to Visit Medellín

Weather

Medellín enjoys a pleasant climate year-round with average temperatures around 72°F (22°C), earning its nickname "City of Eternal Spring."

Best Months

Year-round offers pleasant weather, but December to March and July to August are particularly nice.

Peak Season

December to March brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

April to June and September to November offer lower prices and fewer crowds.
