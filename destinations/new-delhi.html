Discover New Delhi

Welcome to New Delhi, where ancient Mughal monuments meet British colonial architecture and modern Indian energy creates a fascinating capital experience. From the majestic Red Fort to the peaceful Humayun's Tomb, from bustling markets to elegant government buildings, this historic city offers the perfect blend of history, culture, and contemporary Indian life. Best Time to Visit New Delhi

Weather

New Delhi has three main seasons. Winter (November-February) is pleasant, summer (March-June) is hot, and monsoon (July-October) brings rain.

Best Months

October to March offers the best weather with pleasant temperatures.

Peak Season

November to February brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

April to June offers lower prices but extremely hot weather.
