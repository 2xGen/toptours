Best Time to Visit Singapore

Weather

Singapore has a tropical climate with two seasons. Dry season (February-September) brings pleasant weather, while rainy season (October-January) brings monsoon rains.

Best Months

February to September offers the best weather with pleasant conditions.

Peak Season

June to August brings peak tourist season with ideal weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

October to January offers lower prices and fewer crowds, though with more rain.
