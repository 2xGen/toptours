Best Time to Visit Tokyo

Weather

Tokyo enjoys four distinct seasons. Spring (March-May) brings cherry blossoms, summer (June-August) is hot and humid, autumn (September-November) is mild, and winter (December-February) is cool.

Best Months

March to May and September to November offer the best weather with pleasant temperatures.

Peak Season

March to May brings cherry blossom season with beautiful weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

June to August offers lower prices but hot, humid weather with occasional typhoons.
