Best Time to Visit Wellington

Weather

Wellington has a temperate climate. Spring (September-November) and autumn (March-May) are pleasant, summer (December-February) is warm, and winter (June-August) is mild but windy.

Best Months

March to May and September to November offer the best weather with pleasant temperatures.

Peak Season

December to February brings peak tourist season with warm weather, larger crowds, and higher prices.

Off Season

June to August offers lower prices but cooler and windier weather.
