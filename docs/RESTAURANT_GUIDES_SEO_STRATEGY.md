## SEO Optimization Checklist

### ✅ On-Page SEO Elements

1. **Title Tag (H1)**
   - ✅ Contains primary keyword: `[Category] in [Destination]`
   - ✅ Includes year: `2025`
   - ✅ 50-60 characters
   - ✅ Natural, readable format

2. **Meta Description**
   - ✅ 150-160 characters
   - ✅ Includes primary keyword
   - ✅ Includes destination name
   - ✅ Includes call-to-action
   - ✅ Mentions restaurant count and ratings

3. **URL Structure**
   - ✅ Keyword-rich: `/destinations/[id]/restaurants/guides/[category-slug]`
   - ✅ Clean, readable slugs
   - ✅ Consistent format

4. **Heading Hierarchy**
   - ✅ H1: Primary keyword (title)
   - ✅ H2: Keyword variations (sections)
   - ✅ H3: Supporting keywords (subsections)
   - ✅ Natural keyword placement

5. **Content Optimization**
   - ✅ Primary keyword density: 1-2% (8-12 mentions)
   - ✅ Destination name: 15-20 mentions naturally
   - ✅ Long-tail keywords: Naturally integrated
   - ✅ Semantic keywords: dining, eateries, cuisine, culinary
   - ✅ Content length: 2000-3000 words total

6. **Internal Linking**
   - ✅ Links to tour category guides (6+ links)
   - ✅ Links to top tours (6+ links)
   - ✅ Links to main restaurant listing
   - ✅ Links to destination page
   - ✅ Breadcrumb navigation

7. **Structured Data (JSON-LD)**
   - ✅ Article schema
   - ✅ FAQPage schema (8+ FAQs)
   - ✅ ItemList schema (restaurants)
   - ✅ Restaurant schema (individual restaurants)
   - ✅ AggregateRating schema

8. **Images**
   - ✅ Alt text with keywords
   - ✅ Descriptive filenames
   - ✅ Optimized file sizes
   - ✅ Hero image with destination context

## Content Structure for SEO

### Introduction (200-250 words)
- **First sentence:** Primary keyword naturally included
- **Destination name:** 3-4 mentions
- **Long-tail keywords:** 2-3 variations
- **Semantic keywords:** dining scene, culinary, gastronomy
- **User intent:** Answers "what", "why", "where"

### Why Choose Section (4 items)
- **Each item:** Includes destination name + category keyword
- **Headings:** Keyword-rich titles
- **Content:** 2-3 sentences with practical details

### What to Expect Section (6 items)
- **Headings:** Include primary keyword
- **Content:** Detailed, keyword-rich descriptions
- **Destination name:** Naturally included in each item

### Expert Tips (8 tips)
- **Each tip:** Includes destination name
- **Actionable:** Specific restaurant names, neighborhoods
- **Keyword-rich:** Natural keyword variations

### FAQs (8 questions)
- **Questions:** Long-tail keyword questions
- **Answers:** 150-200 words each
- **Destination name:** 2-3 mentions per answer
- **Keywords:** Naturally integrated
- **Schema:** FAQPage structured data

### Restaurant Listings
- **Headings:** "Best [Category] in [Destination]"
- **Descriptions:** Keyword-rich restaurant cards
- **Schema:** ItemList + Restaurant schemas
